About



  • 2012

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Started off as a college motorsports team by a bunch of budding engineers.

  • 2012-2015

    As a Team.

    Participated in various college events.

  • 2015

    Objectives clear, became a startup.

    In due course of time, the objective of the company changed from participating in college events to building a commercial ATV. And, thus, The Road Runners established themselves as a company.

  • 2015 - Present

    As a Startup

    Currently,the company is targeting the relatively unexplored Indian ATV market and at present sells a single seater vehicle. In future, the team intends to develop an inspiring segment of off-road vehicles.
